     It is obvious that the proposed FTC solution is not the
only one that can be used. Rather, many solutions reported
in the literature have the potential to solve the FTC problem,
thanks to the fault-diagnosability and recoverability prop-
erties. This is the core element of the optimization problem
(18). By considering fault-diagnosability and recoverability
properties as constraints into the optimization process of the
thruster-placement problem, we guarantee the existence of
an FTC solution. Thus, the goal of the following is not to                          Fig. 8. Behavior of the attitude tracking error. The 200 faulty
